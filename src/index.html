You turned, looked at me, and said, "Yes?"

With a nervous smile, I said,
"I just had to tell you… you look really cute."

That was it — our first encounter.
So simple, yet it sparked everything that followed.

Obsession With Your Hair

The very first thing I noticed about you wasn’t your smile, your voice, or even your eyes — it was your hair.

You were sitting on the first bench, and I was all the way back on the last. But even from that distance, your hair caught my attention like nothing else. It was perfect. Effortless. Beautiful.

From that day till now, your hair has remained my favorite part of you. I don’t know what it is — maybe the way it moves, maybe the way it frames your face — but I’m obsessed.

Every time I see it, I fall for you all over again.

Growing Closer

We got close so fast — like, really fast.

I’ve never felt anything like it before.
The comfort, the excitement, the way we talked for hours like we’d known each other forever — it all just happened.

No awkward pauses, no overthinking.
Just two people falling into something beautiful, faster than either of us expected.

And honestly, I wouldn’t change a thing.

Creating Memories

We’ve made so many beautiful memories together. From going to the water park with you, to watching movies side by side, to randomly walking the streets with no destination(bht jaada chala deta hu na me) — every single moment felt perfect, just because I was with you. I feel genuinely happy when I’m around you, and these memories mean the world to me. Thank you for everything — for the laughter, the comfort, and the love.
